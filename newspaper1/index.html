Two Pokemon Go players held-up at gunpoint at a Sydney skate park

By Nick Ralston

A man and a woman have been held-up by masked gunman while playing Pokemon Go at a skate park in Sydney's south-west. The pair, both aged 29, drove to the park on Thirlmere Way at Tahmoor at 7pm on Wednesday while playing the mobile app when a white Toyota Hilux reversed across their path, blocking them in the car park.
